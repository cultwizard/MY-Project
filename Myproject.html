About Coldplay

Coldplay is a British rock band formed in London in 1996. The band consists of vocalist and pianist Chris Martin, guitarist Jonny Buckland, bassist Guy Berryman, and drummer Will Champion.

With over 100 million records sold worldwide, Coldplay has established itself as one of the world's best-selling music artists. Their unique sound combines alternative rock, pop, and experimental elements.
